Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. If you request deletion, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

What will be deleted?

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone).
  • Your profile photo and identification documents.
  • Your booking history (excluding financial records required for compliance).
  • Your chat history and ratings.

To request deletion, please click the Request Account Deletion button below or tap the WhatsApp icon at the bottom of the screen to chat with our support team directly.

Our team will process your request within 48 hours.
